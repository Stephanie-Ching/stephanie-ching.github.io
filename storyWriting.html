My Other Lives/ Do You Want to Play a Game?/ The Tainted Blood

I am an interactive game script writer at IGG. I am passionate about storytelling and have written two romance stories so far - Do You Want to Play a Game and The Tainted Blood. Writing is not just my job, it's also my hobby. In my spare time, I enjoy writing short stories and exploring different genres. One of my recent works is a short story called My Other Lives, which I wrote in 2021. I am always looking for new opportunities to write and create engaging stories, and I am excited to see where my writing journey takes me next.
